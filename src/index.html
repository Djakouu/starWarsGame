Rules of the Game:

You are

and your goal is to tear down all your enemies within 2 minutes flat


Your enemies are : Anakin

Once you catch them you mark 3 points and they vanish


Your worst enemy is DarthVader




Try to catch it and you will lose 4 points. Plus, he is Immortal


If you do not exterminate your enemies before the time is up you lose




If you do exterminate them within the time your final score will be:




The points marked + the third of the remaining time




You got it! 3 seconds are worht 1 point




If your final score is negative or equals to zero, you lose




If it is posivite, you can guess, you win!


Examples:



You marked 10 points and the remaining time is 30 seconds


Your final score is (10 + 30/3) = 20 points




You marked -8 points and the remaining time is 35 seconds


Your final score is (-8 + 35/3) = 3 points




You marked -4 points and the remaining time is 10 seconds


Your final score is (-4 + 10/3) = -1 point
